# Forge Quantum-inspired refinement

## Goal

Make Forge feel like a premium, product-first development agency while keeping its white liquid-glass identity and supplied business copy.

## Acceptance criteria

- All body, heading, label, form, and CTA text remains readable on its surface.
- Add original equivalents of premium agency patterns: active sticky-process progression, section-aware navigation, solution-story carousel, restrained appear effects, and responsive form interactions.
- Preserve the real mailto contact flow and avoid invented client names, testimonials, metrics, case studies, blog posts, or search results.
- Preserve reduced-motion behavior and keyboard-operable navigation, carousel, FAQ, and form controls.

## Non-goals

- Copying QuantumFlux code, assets, wording, layouts, CMS structure, or animation choreography.
- Adding empty CMS, blog, careers, or work routes without real Forge source material.
